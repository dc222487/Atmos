# Atmos — Your Weather, Styled

**Atmos** is a stylish, modern weather web app that does more than show you the temperature — it sets the vibe!

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Smart City Search:** Get instant city suggestions as you type with live geocoding.
- 🎨 **Dynamic Themes:** Beautiful background gradients that change with the weather.
- 🌦️ **Weather Animations:** Sun rays, moving clouds, and rain effects that bring the weather to life.
- 🧭 **Outfit & Activity Tips:** Personalized suggestions for what to wear and the best time to go outside.
- 🔭 **Stargazing Forecast:** Know if tonight’s good for catching stars.
- 🌍 **Automatic Units:** Detects your region to show temperatures in °C or °F automatically.
- 🎨 **Custom Mouse Trail:** A fun interactive cursor effect follows you around.
- 📱 **Responsive Design:** Looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — semantic structure  
- **CSS3** — responsive design, custom animations & effects  
- **JavaScript (Vanilla)** — dynamic UI, API calls, interactivity  
- **APIs:**
  - [Visual Crossing Weather API](https://www.visualcrossing.com/) — accurate weather data
  - [Open-Meteo Geocoding API](https://open-meteo.com/) — city name suggestions

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Type your location:** Instant suggestions help you pick the right place.
2. **Get the vibe:** Atmos pulls live weather and gives you a clear snapshot.
3. **See the forecast:** View a stylish 7-day outlook.
4. **Dress smart:** Outfit tips help you plan your day.
5. **Enjoy the view:** Relax with smooth weather-themed animations and interactive effects.
